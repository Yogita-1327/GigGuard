Frequently Asked Questions
Who is eligible for GigGuard?▾
Any active delivery partner on Swiggy, Zomato, Blinkit, Dunzo, Zepto, or BigBasket operating in our covered cities. You need at least 30 deliveries in the past month and an active UPI-linked bank account.
How is my premium calculated?▾
Your weekly premium is a percentage of your declared daily earnings — 3.75% for High Risk cities, 2.5% for Medium Risk, and 1.25% for Low Risk. Risk category is determined by your city's disruption index, updated monthly.
What counts as a disruption?▾
Qualifying disruptions: heavy rainfall (above 25mm/hr), flash floods, AQI above 300, area road blockages, declared public strikes, extreme heat above 44°C. Must be verified by two independent data sources.
Can I claim multiple times in a week?▾
Yes. Your policy covers up to 3 disruption events per week, with a maximum total payout of 2× your daily coverage per week. Each event must be a separately verified disruption of at least 2 hours.
Is my data safe?▾
All data is encrypted with 256-bit AES. We are an IRDAI Sandbox Partner compliant with DPDP Act 2023. Your earnings data is never sold and only used for risk calculation and claim processing.
